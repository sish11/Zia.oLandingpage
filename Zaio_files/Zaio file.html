FAQ

Is the program accredited?

YES! All work readiness programs that are curated by Zaio are accredited with the South African MICT SETA

How long is the work-readiness program?

We help each organization through the process of deciding how long it should take by factoring the demographics and skill sets of learners and what skills need to be imparted during the program. Each program can be as short as 6 weeks or as long as two years; dependent on your needs.

How many learners go through the program?

The number of candidates is tailored to your needs, with a minimum of 5 candidates.

How are soft skills fostered in the program?

Candidates are put into teams to work projects. Throughout this exercise candidates are expected to communicate their work to their team members, they are expected to brainstorm and solve problems that they may encounter later down the projects timeline together as a team. Deliverables will be set for the project and the team will have to work together to divide the workload to meet deadlines hence encouraging students to be a team player and improve their time and resource management.

Can my organisation benefit from SDL and BEE points?

YES! We are a level 4 BBBEE company. Through our training, we help companies spend their SDL funds to transform their digital strategies with a relevant pool of talent. To maximise SDL and BBBEE points with Zaio even further, you can contact the Youth Employment Services who we have partnered with to teach technical skills around the county.

What is the cost to my organisation?

The cost is dependent on the final customized journey. Talk to an expert to find out more.
